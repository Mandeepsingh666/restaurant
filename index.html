About Us

Welcome to PNK Surinamese Cuisine & Majnoon Café — a family-owned restaurant bringing the rich, vibrant flavors of Suriname to Richmond Hill, New York. Our kitchen celebrates the unique cultural blend that makes Surinamese cuisine one of the world's most extraordinary: Indonesian Javanese traditions, Caribbean soul, and multicultural heritage — all crafted with love and authentic recipes passed down through generations.

Every dish is prepared fresh daily using time-honored recipes and 100% Halal ingredients. From our signature Javaanse Nasi and Bami to our soul-warming Saoto Soup, every bite tells the story of Suriname's rich multicultural tapestry.
